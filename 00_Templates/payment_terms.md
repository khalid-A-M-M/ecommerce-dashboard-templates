# Payment Terms

## Standard Terms

The project starts after receiving 50 percent upfront payment.

The remaining 50 percent is due after the dashboard is demonstrated and before final access, files, or repository permissions are released.

## Payment Method

Preferred method: PayPal Business Invoice.

Do not use personal transfers for client work. An invoice creates a clearer paper trail for both sides.

## Scope Changes

Any request outside the agreed package is treated as an additional item and quoted before implementation.

## Refund Policy

The upfront payment reserves project time and covers discovery, setup, and initial implementation. It is not refundable after work starts.

If a technical blocker makes the original scope impossible, both sides agree on either a revised scope or partial delivery based on completed work.
