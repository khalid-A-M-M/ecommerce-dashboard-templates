# Client Onboarding Questions

## Business

1. What platform does your store use? Salla, Zid, Shopify, WooCommerce, or other?
2. What are the top 3 numbers you check every week?
3. Who will use the dashboard?
4. Do you need Arabic, English, or both?

## Data Sources

1. Where are orders stored today?
2. Do you have API access?
3. Do you track product cost per item?
4. Do you track ad spend? If yes, where?
5. Do you have refunds, cancellations, or partial payments?

## KPI Definitions

1. How do you define net revenue?
2. How do you define profit?
3. Should shipping fees be included in revenue?
4. Should discounts be deducted before or after revenue reporting?
5. What date should reports use: order date, payment date, or fulfillment date?

## Access Needed

- Store API key or export sample.
- Product cost sheet.
- Ad spend export or account access.
- Logo and preferred brand colors, if available.

## Approval

Please confirm:

- The selected package.
- The dashboard pages needed.
- The upfront payment has been sent.
- The data access method is ready.
